CLASS 1 MELT PROGRAM

Course Overview

Barnala Driver Training Academy's Class 1 MELT (Mandatory Entry-Level Training) program prepares students with the practical skills, safety knowledge, and confidence required to begin a professional commercial driving career in Manitoba.

Career Ready

WHAT YOU'LL LEARN

Training Modules

Our Class 1 MELT program provides the knowledge and practical skills needed to become a safe and professional commercial truck driver.

Classroom Training

Learn traffic laws, regulations, vehicle systems and professional driver responsibilities.

Pre-Trip Inspection

Master complete commercial vehicle inspections required by Manitoba standards.

Backing Skills

Practice straight backing, offset backing, alley dock and coupling procedures.

Road Driving
