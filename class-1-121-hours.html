FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Questions About the 121.5-Hour Program

How long does it take to get a Class 1 licence in Manitoba?

With our 121.5-hour program, you can complete your training in just a few weeks depending on your schedule and availability.

Is the 121.5-hour program MELT-approved?

This program is designed for students who meet the exemption criteria or already have prior training. If you need the full MELT program, we also offer the 244-hour Class 1 course.

Can I work outside Manitoba with a Class 1 licence?

Yes. A Manitoba Class 1 licence is recognized across Canada and accepted by many transportation companies.

What's the difference between this and the 244-hour program?

The 121.5-hour course is a streamlined option for eligible students, while the 244-hour MELT program is required for most new commercial drivers.
