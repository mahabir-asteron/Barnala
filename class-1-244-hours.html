ABOUT MELT

What Is the MELT Program?

The 244-hour Class 1 MELT (Mandatory Entry-Level Training) program is required for new Class 1 commercial driver's licence applicants in Manitoba. It provides comprehensive classroom instruction, practical yard exercises, and extensive on-road driving experience to prepare students for a successful trucking career.

🚛 Vehicle Operation

Master safe operation and complete control of commercial trucks.

🛡 Road Safety

Learn defensive driving, emergency response, and hazard awareness.

📋 Regulations

Understand Manitoba transportation laws, inspections, and compliance.

🎯 Professional Skills

Develop the confidence and professionalism employers expect.
