CLASS 1 MELT PROGRAM

Course Overview

Our 244-hour Class 1 MELT Program is Manitoba's complete Mandatory Entry-Level Training course designed for new commercial drivers. Students receive classroom instruction, practical yard training, and extensive on-road experience to become safe, confident, and job-ready professional truck drivers.
