CLASS 5 LICENSE

What Is a Class 5 License?

A Class 5 license is the most common type of driver's license in Manitoba. It allows you to drive:

  • Passenger vehicles (cars, SUVs, minivans)
  • Light trucks and utility vehicles
  • Small delivery vehicles (non-commercial)
GDL PROGRAM

Manitoba's Graduated Driver Licensing (GDL) System

We support you through all three stages of GDL:

Learner Stage (L)

Written test preparation, traffic rules, and basic vehicle handling.

Intermediate Stage (I)

In-depth training, road safety, hazard perception, and city driving.

Full License (F)

Advanced driving techniques, highway training, winter driving, and more.
