ELIGIBILITY REQUIREMENTS

Click Any Requirement

Applicants must demonstrate sufficient English language proficiency to understand classroom instruction, safety regulations, traffic signs, written examinations, and communicate effectively with instructors, employers, and emergency personnel. Good communication skills are essential for safe commercial vehicle operation.

Students must successfully complete the Manitoba Air Brake (Q) Endorsement. Training includes air brake system operation, inspections, brake adjustments, safety procedures, and the required written and practical examinations.

Applicants must hold a valid Manitoba Class 5 Full Driver's Licence. Learner licences or suspended licences are not eligible for Class 1 training.

Students must meet Manitoba commercial driver medical standards and complete any required medical examination before licensing.

Students must successfully complete the Manitoba Class 1 Knowledge Test before taking the practical road examination.
