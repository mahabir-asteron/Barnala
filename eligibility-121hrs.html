ELIGIBILITY

Eligibility Requirements

Language Proficiency

You must be able to understand English well enough to read road signs, follow safety instructions, and communicate effectively.

Air Brake Endorsement

Students must successfully complete the Air Brake (Q) endorsement as part of the Class 1 licensing process.

Driver's Licence

A valid Manitoba Class 5 Full driver's licence is required before beginning Class 1 training.

Medical Fitness

Applicants must pass a medical examination and meet Manitoba's commercial driver medical standards.

Knowledge Test

Students must pass the Class 1 knowledge test before taking the practical road test.
