ELIGIBILITY REQUIREMENTS

Everything You Need Before You Apply

Before enrolling in the Class 1 (244 Hours) Training Program, make sure you meet the eligibility requirements established for commercial driver training in Manitoba. These requirements help ensure students are prepared to complete the program safely and successfully.

Age

Applicants must generally be at least 18 years of age to apply for a Class 1 commercial driver's licence in Manitoba.

Air Brake Endorsement

The Class 1 (244 Hours) program includes comprehensive air brake training, helping students develop the practical knowledge and skills required to safely operate commercial vehicles equipped with air brake systems.
