ELIGIBILITY REQUIREMENTS

Are You Eligible for Class 3 Training?

Before enrolling in the Class 3 Driver Training Program, make sure you meet the following eligibility requirements.

Knowledge Test

You must pass a written knowledge test on traffic laws, safe driving practices, and specific regulations related to Class 3 vehicles.

Air Brake Endorsement

Complete the Air Brake Endorsement course required for operating commercial vehicles equipped with air brake systems.

Driver's License

You need a valid Class 5 (Full) driver's license or an equivalent licence before applying.

Medical Fitness

You must pass a medical examination to ensure you meet the physical requirements for driving a commercial vehicle.
