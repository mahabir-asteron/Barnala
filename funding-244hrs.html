Government Funding Process

Follow These 4 Easy Steps

Complete the following requirements before applying for the Government Sponsored Class 1 MELT Training Program.

01

Class 1 Knowledge Test & Medical

Complete the Class 1 knowledge test and undergo a medical examination. These are mandatory requirements to qualify for the MELT program.

02

Meet with Manitoba Jobs and Skills Development Centre

Schedule a meeting with a representative from a Manitoba Jobs and Skills Development Centre. They will provide guidance on available funding options and eligibility criteria for the MELT program.

03

Secure a Conditional Offer of Employment (COE)

Obtain a Conditional Offer of Employment from a prospective employer in the trucking industry. This demonstrates your commitment and is required for Government funding.

04

Obtain a Letter of Acceptance

Using your Conditional Offer of Employment, obtain a Letter of Acceptance from an approved private vocational institution or driver training school offering the MELT program.
